How to add a promotional overview field

Sometimes it may be useful to put a short paragraph onto your page, describing what the page is for or offering some marketing or promotional content. This paragraph is usually placed in the top left of the page, where visitors can easily see it. It might contain persuasive information, dates that are important to remember, or other content you want to highlight.

When editing your page, the 'overview' field in the 'Promotional fields' vertical tab allows you to add this content. By default, it is placed above the body of your page in the 'full' layout'.

If you need to place it by hand:

  1. Add your content to the 'Overview' field on the 'Promotional fields' vertical tab and save your page.
  2. On the Panelizer tab, in the 'content' area, choose the region where you want to place the overview field. Click the gear in the top left corner, then 'Add content'.
  3. In the modal window that appears, click 'node' on the left-side tabs, then click on 'Field: Overview'.
  4. Change the 'Label' field to 'hidden', to prevent a label from showing on this field in your page. If you want to add a header above the overview field, check the 'title override' checkbox and type your header text in the 'Title' field. Click 'Continue' and then 'Finish'.
  5. Click 'Save' at the bottom left, and then click on the 'View' tab to see how it looks.
