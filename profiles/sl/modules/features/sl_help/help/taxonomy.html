Detailed Overview the Taxonomies in Use on the Hartnell College website

On this page you will find a glossary of Taxonomies in use on the site and a brief description of how they are used. Taxonomy is the practice of classifying things. In Drupal, the Taxonomy module is how you classify your website content, and it is an important piece of many sites' information architecture.

It's helpful to think of taxonomy as the use of "categories". In fact, this is what it was called in earlier versions of Drupal. Taxonomy lets you gather together content under one term or another. It has become advanced enough to give you as much flexibility as you need in designing classification schemes.

Taxonomies

  • Event Type
    • Used to categorize the 5 type of events that appear on the calendar and event feeds.
  • Audience
    • Used to target the demographic for which this content is intended.
  • Location Type
    • This is used to categorize campus, building, lab, office, or community locations.
  • Testimonial type
    • Used to categorize the types of featured testimonial blocks.
  • Athletic Teams
    • This vocabulary is used in the multiple content types "News, Events, Flexible pages, Person" to differentiate between the teams this content relates.
  • Academic Subject or Major
    • Used in the "Slideshow" content type to categorize the which academic field of Hartnell College this falls under.
  • Editorial Site Section
    • The section of the site where this content belongs. Controls editorial access to creating and editing content (Workbench Access)
  • Page Type
    • Similar to the "Audience" vocabulary, this is used to create top level pages for "flexible page" content types.
  • Person
    • This vocabulary is used in the "person" content type to categorize faculty, staff, students and athletes.
  • Department
    • This vocabulary is associated with the "Flexible" content type is related to Feature Links and how they are filtered to related pages and is used as a secondary level of categorization..
  • News
    • This is used to categorize articles and or press releases in the news feeds by Term.
  • Promotion Type
    • This is used to categorize and style the different promotional content types "Button, Ad, and Alert" .
  • FAQ
    • This is used to categorize frequently asked questions by Term.
