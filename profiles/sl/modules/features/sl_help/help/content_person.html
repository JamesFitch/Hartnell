Adding a Person

  1. Click on "Add content" in the admin menu.
  2. Click on "Person"
  3. Fill out the appropriate "Prefix"
  4. Fill out the "First name" (this is required)
  5. Fill out the "Last name" (also required)
  6. Fill out the "Credentials"
  7. Upload a small photo in "Headshot" (which will be resized to maximum of 300 by 350 pixels).
  8. Select the "Person Type" from the pulldown menu.
  9. Fill out the "Role or Job Title"
  10. Fill out the "Department"
  11. Fill out the "Expertise"
  12. Fill out the "Location" and add a location with the "Create Campus location" if you must.
  13. Fill out the "Room"
  14. Fill out the "Phone number"
  15. Fill out the "Fax"
  16. Fill out the "Email"
  17. And finally, fill out the "Bio", but keep it short and sweet! This field will only hold 140 characters.
