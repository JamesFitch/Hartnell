To place an Audience Promo Item on the Home Page:
  • Browse to the Promotion item's Edit tab.
  • Set:
    • Ad Type to "Promotion"
    • Audience to one of the audiences included in the Home Page Audience Promotions feature's tabs menu.
      (Configuring Audience Promotion tabs)
  • Set Publishing Options -> Promoted to front page
  • Important: Only 3 Promotion items per Audience will be displayed. When replacing a Promotion item be sure to disable the one being replaced by either
    • Un-checking its "Promoted to front page" field
    • Setting its "Audience" field to "None"
