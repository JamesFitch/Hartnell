Admin: Assigning people to Site Sections

Go to "Configuration" in the Administrative Menu, then Workbench, Workbench Access.

The opening tab is "Editors". To assign someone to a particular area, click on that area name. You'll see the editors who are already assigned, and you can assign someone else by typing in their username. Be sure to click "Update editors" before leaving the page.

You can also assign everyone who is a particular "Role". Simply select the appropriate role(s), and then "Update roles".
