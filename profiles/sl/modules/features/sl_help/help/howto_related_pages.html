How to add a list of Related Pages

You can list other pages on the Hartnell site which are related to this one by using the related pages field. While editing your page, switch to the 'Related items' vertical tab, and use the 'Related Pages' field to reference the pages you're interested in.

Start typing in the field, and the titles of pages matching your search will appear. Click on the page you want to select it, and then repeat as many times as needed for other related pages.

Click save when you're done adding pages.

Using the 'full' layout, your list of related pages will show up at the bottom of your content in a two-column grid, like this:

If you need to add it by hand, you can do so in Panelizer:

  1. Choose the region you want to put your related pages in
  2. Click the gear in the top left corner of that region and then click 'Add content'.
  3. Choose 'Node' from the tabs to the left, and then click on 'Field: Related Flexpages (field_related_flexpages)'.
  4. You can Check Override Title and customize this pane.
  5. For Label select 'Pane title'
  6. For Select a formatter select 'Label'
  7. Click 'Continue' at the bottom left of the pane to move to the next page.
  8. Check 'Link label to the referenced entity ' at the top left corner.
  9. Click 'Finish' at the bottom left of the pane.
  10. Click 'Save' at the bottom left of the page
  11. Use the 'View' tab at the top right to see how it looks.
