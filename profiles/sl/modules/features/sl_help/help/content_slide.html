Creating a Slide Collection

A Slide Collection is an awful lot like a Slideshow. A slideshow is a series of single images, or slides, displayed in order. These images can be linked to content on your website.

  1. Click on "Add content" in the admin menu.
  2. Click on "Slide Collection"
  3. Fill out the "Title" (this is required).
  4. Begin with your first slide.
    1. Upload your first image. You can "Browse" your computer until you find your image, then click "Upload">
    2. Fill out the first "Title", and "Description"
    3. The Title next to URL can be ignored, but if you'd like to link, the URL is ready to accomodate.
