Overview: CWI Newsletter

The CWI Newsletter is controlled by the Simplenews Module. When you create a CWI Newsletter, it is published to the site as a page just like other types of content, but with this content type, you also have the option to send it out to a mailing list.

Newsletter Home

Newsletter Home—contains subscribe form, current issue teaser, previous issues, and links to monthly archives.

Create CWI Newsletter

Create CWI Newsletter—form to create a new issue of the CWI Newsletter. CWI Newsletters contain a title, body, and optionally news item(s). See Creating a Newsletter for an explanation of fields.

Newsletter Categories

Newsletter Categories—a College of Western Idaho newsletter category has been set up for use. Other newsletter categories, perhaps for different departments, may be added by administrators of the newsletter.

Settings for Newsletters

Newsletter Settings—Update these settings to configure default e-mail addresses for test newsletter sending, sender information, the "From:" e-mail address and other settings. These options will be the defaults for new newsletters, but can be overridden in the newsletter editing form.

Subscription and Confirmation

Subscription Settings—Specify confirmation messages, pages and actions that should happen when someone subscribes to the newsletter.

Send mail

Send mail settings for newsletters—Configure the technical details of how the newsletter e-mails should be sent.

Manage Subscribers

To update your subscriber list, go to admin/people/simplenews or in the top-most administration menu, navigate to People and then click on the Newsletter Subscriptions tab in the upper right corner.
