Creating styled buttons

  1. Type your text for your button into the Body textbox, and select it. a screenshot of the Body textbox.
  2. Make it into a link by clicking the Link button and selecting either an internal location, an email address or a URL, and then selecting or typing in the link.
  3. Then with the linked text selected, click Styles, and select one of the button types. Promo Button is for putting on dark backgrounds; Gold, Burgundy, and Black are for universal use; otherwise, the buttons will be as long as they're allowed to be.
  4. Once you've applied the style, it will not look any different in your edit screen. But Preview or Save it, and you'll see the style:

  5. Now let's add an icon. First, in another tab in your browser, go to Font Icons in your admin menu.
  6. This is the Hartnell Font icons page. and choose which icon you'd like to use. Select the text next to the icon, and copy it (Ctrl+C).
  7. Switch back to your button page. Click on the Source button.
