Adding a department

  1. Click on "Add content" in the admin menu.
  2. Click on "Department"
  3. In "Department Name" enter the name of the department.
  4. In "Address" enter the street or mailing address of the department.
  5. For "Building" choose the building in which the department resides.
  6. In "Room" enter the room number/name.
  7. In the "Phone numbers" fields enter up to two phone numbers for the department.
  8. In "Fax" fill in the fax number.
  9. In "Email" fill in the email address for the department. (The website will output it as a mailto link).
  10. Unless instructed otherwise, do not enter anything into "Tags."

  11. If you are updating the content, before saving go to the "Revision information" fieldset.

    Ensure the "Create new revision" option is checked and then provide a brief explanation of your changes.

  12. When you have finished adding or updating the content, press "Save" at the bottom of the page.
  13. Note that the "Preview" functionality is limited and will not completely display how your content will look once published.
