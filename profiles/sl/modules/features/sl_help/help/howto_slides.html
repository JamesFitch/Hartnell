How to place promotional slides on your internal pages

There are two ways to post a slideshow or slides on your pages:

  1. Create new content, Slide Collection, or
  2. you can create a slideshow right on the page you're editing, by going to Images in the vertical tabs, and clicking on + Create Slide Collection.

Here's how it goes, either way

  1. Add a title (this is mandatory)
  2. Then, begin creating slides:
    • Upload an image
    • Give it a title
    • Give it a description
    • Give it a URL
    • Click Add Another Item to repeat
    • Repeat until you have all the slides you want
  3. You can rearrange slides by dragging up and down on the cross-hair handles.
  4. Click Save

If you know the name of a slideshow, you can start typing its name in the Slideshow textbox in the Images vertical tab of the page you'd like to add it to.

Design Note: Slideshows are designed to fill the entire width of the page. They can fit in smaller size but you may loose content or copy in the process.
