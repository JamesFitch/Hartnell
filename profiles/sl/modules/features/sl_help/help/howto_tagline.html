How to add a 'related page tagline'

On the home page (and possibly elsewhere), your page might be featured. The page icon and page title will be used to create a button. In order to provide more context, you can provide a tagline.

As placed on the home page, the button will look like this - the tagline field is highlighted:

While editing your page, you can supply this tagline by clicking the 'Call to Action' tab at the left, and then filling out the 'Tagline' field.
