How to add slideshow content to your home page

To make a slide collection from scratch >> Select Add Content >> Homepage Slide Collection

First let's review the anatomy of the homepage slideshow, based on the fields you will fill out for the Homepage Slide Collection content type. You can have up to ten slides per content type.

  1. This is the image. The perfect upload size for this 1180 pixels by 490 pixels.
  2. This is your title area, for best usage one line titles are recommended.
  3. This is the description of your first homepage slide, your body copy should go here.
  4. The more information or URL field is for your slide link. You can use an internal or external link here. Clicking on this link or the image will redirect to your link.
  5. This is the Academic Area or Major field, the Academic Area or Major will be the headline for the thumbnail located below this slide .

To publish your content type slideshow to the home page. You will need to have selected both Published and Promoted to front page. While you can created mulitple home page slideshows (planning for future promotions events etc.) you should only publish one at a time. Don't forget to click the "Save" button to preserve your changes.
