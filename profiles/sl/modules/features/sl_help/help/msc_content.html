Adding content

Available content types

  • Department
  • Event
  • Flexible page
  • News
  • Virtual tour location
  • Webform

Department

Departments provide contact info to visitors and get displayed on internal directory pages.

Events

Events get placed in the school calendar and appear in various context-specific lists around the website. They have starting and ending times, a location, plus several ways to categorize them, such as audience and event type. You can attach related files and links, a contact person, and a sign-up link.

Flexible page

Flexible pages comprise most of the site. They have many options and features, making them powerful.

Some of the features include:

  • Categorizing by site section and audience
  • Header banner and/or slideshow
  • Rich text promotional fields, used for secondary content, call-to-actions, additional info, button links, etc.
  • Related links and contact people
  • The ability to switch layouts and move content around within the layout (panelizer)

News

News items serve as announcements or pseudo blog posts.

Virtual tour location

Use virtual tours to provide additional information about your school and its campus using text, images, and media.

Webform

Webforms enable site builders to create forms that visitors can fill out. You can add several types of fields, including plain text fields as well as select options and email fields.

Webforms collect the submitted data in a table, and you can also set them up to email various custom recipients when a form is submitted. This is useful for contact forms, for example.

Using the rich text editor

The rich text editor allows users to add custom styling to the text they enter into fields. Some of the styling is basic, such as bolding and italicizing. Some of it changes the underlying markup, such giving a plain list bullets or numbers. On this site, some of the options are more advanced, allowing you to incorporate reusable styles. (See the Reusable styles help page for a comprehensive list of available options.)
