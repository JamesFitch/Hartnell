Adding a webform

Creating a webform is a multi-step process. First you need to create the page in which it lives; second you need to create the form; third you need to configure the form settings, such as emails and form submit behavior.

Creating the page

  1. Click on "Add content" in the admin menu.
  2. Click on "Webform"
  3. In "Title" enter the name or title of the form.

  4. In the "Banner Image" field, browse to the image you would like to display at the top of the page and then click the "Upload" button.

    Note that your image will be resized and then cropped to 915x300 pixels regardless of its original dimensions. So you will want to upload an image that has those dimensions already or is larger.

  5. Under "Audience" choose the appropriate audience for this event.
  6. In "Site Section," choose the appropriate place for this news item to appear.
  7. Unless instructed otherwise, do not enter anything into "Tags."
  8. In "Body," input the description of the event. This is a rich text field so you can add various styles and insert images and videos. To see how to use rich text, visit this help page: Using the rich text editor.

  9. Using the "Department" field you can associate departments with the form. Pick the name of the "Department" out of the dropdown list.

  10. If you are updating an event, before saving go to the "Revision information" fieldset.

    Ensure the "Create new revision" option is checked and then provide a brief explanation of your changes.

  11. When you have finished adding or updating the content, press "Save" at the bottom of the page.
  12. Note that the "Preview" functionality is limited and will not completely display how your content will look once published.

Creating the form

  1. After creating the page, click on its "Webform" tab.
  2. "Components" are the pieces that make up your form. For example a "name" or "email" field, a page break, or select options. To add a new component, fill out its Label, choose what type of component it should be, choose whether or not it should be mandatory, and then click the "Add" button.
  3. On the resulting component settings page, use the help tips to set your preferences, then click "Save component."
  4. For more information on how to create and use Drupal webforms, see this official documentation page: Webform Module
