Adding a news item

  1. Click on "Add content" in the admin menu.
  2. Click on "News"
  3. In "Title" enter the name or title of the news post.
  4. If you have a graphic to share, you can upload it in "News Hero".

  5. If the publish date should be different than the default (which is when this content was created), enter it in "Publish Date."

    To choose the day, click on the "Date" field.

    A little calendar will pop up, allowing you to choose the correct day.

    To choose the time, click on the "Time" field.

    You may have to click twice to activate it. You can use the keyboard arrows to choose the time, or you can select each element (hour, minute, am/pm) and type it in.

  6. Is this an emergency announcement? If so, check the "Emergency" box and this news item will appear at the top of lists.
  7. Select what type of news or blog this is with the "Type of News" pulldown. By selecting the type of news you will indicating what page you would like feed this content through.
  8. Under "Audience" choose the appropriate audience for this event.
  9. In "Site Section," choose the appropriate place for this news item to appear.
  10. Unless instructed otherwise, do not enter anything into "Tags."
  11. In "Body," input the description of the event. This is a rich text field so you can add various styles and insert images and videos. To see how to use rich text, visit this help page: Using the rich text editor.

  12. If you are updating an event, before saving go to the "Revision information" fieldset.

    Ensure the "Create new revision" option is checked and then provide a brief explanation of your changes.

  13. When you have finished adding or updating the content, press "Save" at the bottom of the page.
  14. Note that the "Preview" functionality is limited and will not completely display how your content will look once published.
