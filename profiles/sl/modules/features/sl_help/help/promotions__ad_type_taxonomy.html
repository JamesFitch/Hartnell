Promotion Content Types and the "Ad Type" Taxonomy

The "Ad Type" taxonomy can be used to organize and group "Promotion" content items.

Three categories have been pre-configured:

"Button"

-- clickable graphical buttons/links
(Each of the items in the screenshot--"Contact", "Maps", "Phone"--represents a Promotion content item.)

"Promotion"

An ad or promotion, for display in sidebars and similar content areas.

"Alert"

Similar to the Promotion, this ad style has slightly different styling as a call to action for display in sidebars and similar content areas.
