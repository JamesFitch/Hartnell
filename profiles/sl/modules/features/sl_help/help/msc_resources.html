External resources

There are lots of resources available throughout the internet that can help you learn more about Drupal. Some of them cost money, but many are free. Here's a list of places to check out, to get you started:

  • Drupal.org: The hub of all things Drupal, you can find answers to all sorts of questions here, as well as documentation and tutorials. Tip: Do a search before you ask a question. (Free)
  • NodeOne Learning Library: The team at NodeOne has put out several series of video tutorials that range from beginner to advanced. (Free)
  • Lullabot: Lullabot has focused on Drupal training and education for several years, and they have released quite a few free videos, podcasts, and blog posts that detail how to accomplish various tasks in Drupal. (Free)
  • Drupalize.me: This is a paid service by Lullabot that provides hundreds of hours of video training, ranging from beginner to advanced. (Paid)
  • LevelTen: LevelTen offers a collection of helpful blog posts, articles, podcasts, and whitepapers. Of note is their Getting Started with Drupal 7 tutorial series. (Free)

As you browse and search documentation and tutorials, keep in mind that this site uses Drupal 7, and that documentation for other versions may not apply.
