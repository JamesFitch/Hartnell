How to add a contact person to your page

You can easily include contact information for your page. The normal place for this contact info is in the sidebar, or at the bottom of your page, and it looks something like this:

While editing your page, switch to the 'Related Items' vertical tab and scroll to the Contact Person form.

You'll see a field marked 'Contact Person'. Typing in that field will search for people in the faculty/staff directory - click the name you want from the search results, and save your page.

If you need to place your contact person(s) by hand onto the page, you can do so by:

  1. In the Panelizer | Content screen for your page, choose the bottom region and click the gear | add content.
  2. Choose 'Node' from the left side tabs, and click 'Field: Contact person: (field_reference_person)'
  3. Click 'Finish'
  4. Click 'Save' at the bottom-left of the screen.
  5. Click the 'view' tab to see how it looks
