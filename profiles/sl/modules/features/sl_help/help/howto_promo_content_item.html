How to add a reusable promotional item to your flexible page

You can easily include promotional items to your page. The place for a promo is in the sidebar or at the bottom of your page, and it looks something like this:

While editing your page, switch to the 'Promotional Fields' vertical tab and scroll to the top of the form.

You'll see a field marked 'Promo Content Items'. Typing in that field will search for promo in the available Promotion content types - click the name you want from the search results, and save your page.

In the full layout, your Promotion person will automatically show. However, if you need to place them by hand onto the page, you can do so by:

  1. In the Panelizer | Content screen for your page, choose the bottom region and click the gear | add content.

  3. Click 'Finish'
  4. Click 'Save' at the bottom-left of the screen.
