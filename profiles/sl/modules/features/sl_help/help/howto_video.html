How to add a video to your page

When editing a page, your video can be placed in any rich text field (such as the body or promotional fileds) by clicking the "Embed Video" Button.

The 'Video' field takes the embed iframe info and turns it into a video embedded directly in your page. Just paste the info in and save your video will be shown above the body of your page.

If using YouTube this feature works best with these embed settings.
