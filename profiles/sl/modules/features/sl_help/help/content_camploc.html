Creating a Campus Location

Campus Location is a content type that only contains locations on campus.

Most of the necessary fields are in the "Standards fields" tab.

  1. Fill in the "Location Name". This is a required field.
  2. Select the proper "Location Type" .
  3. Upload the "Location Photo".
  4. Fill in the full mailing Street Address" If your event occurs at a location with a physical address, and you'd like to provide a map, enter the address in a single line: 123 Main Street Anywhere, USA 12345. This will generate a link to google maps..
  5. Fill in the "Building Name " If your event occurs at a campus specific building with a recognizable name, enter the name in a single line: Learning Resource Center.
  6. Fill in the "City".
  7. Fill in the "State".
  8. Fill in the "Zip"code.
  9. Fill in the "Phone" number, including area code.
  10. Fill in the "Description". This could include what departments are housed in the building, hours ofS operation, when the trees were planted in the arboretum, or any other special facts about the location.
