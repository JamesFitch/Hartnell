How to Navigate Frequently asked Questions

Create FAQ Content Type or Node:

It's fairly simple to get a working FAQ page.

  1. To create FAQ nodes at "Add content >> FAQ", you will need to choose an FAQ term and ensure your content is published.
  2. To see if users have the correct permissions to see FAQ nodes at "Administration Menu >> People >> Permissions"
  3. The FAQs should appear on the FAQ page "/faq "

FAQ Features: Main Page

Located on the master Frequently Asked Questions page, this view features a list of taxonomy driven FAQ categories and allows instant access to individual FAQ category pages. For example the FAQ page for Parents.

Also located on the master Frequently Asked Questions page, this view features a list of all frequently asked questions and teaser answers which are grouped by their categories. Clicking on the question directs you to the full questions and with complete answer. The search feature allows you to filter the questions and answers by either a key word or phrase and/or by topic.

FAQ Features: Category Pages

Similar to the master page, the individual topic pages search feature allows you to filter the questions by either a key word or phrase. There is an additional view on this page titled "Popular FAQ Topics" which filters the most popular views by those with the most flags or "Likes".
